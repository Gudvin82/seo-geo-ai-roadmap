# Discoverability Coverage

`v3.7.0` adds a broader discoverability coverage layer on top of the scanner
foundation.

Included modules:

- RU and AI bot policy, including `YandexAdditional`
- `ai.txt` validation and contradiction review
- schema coverage with `WebSite` support
- FAQ and answer-ready detection
- Open Graph and Twitter Card completeness
- `robots.txt` ↔ sitemap linkage

Honest limitations:

- some checks are heuristic
- `ai.txt` is still an emergent convention
- bot policy checks do not prove inclusion in answer engines
- social metadata checks do not prove real rendering in every client
