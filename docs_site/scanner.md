# Scanner Intake

`v3.6.0` adds a dedicated self-hosted/public-ready scanner intake layer.

What it includes:

- dedicated intake page
- passive, active, and feature-flagged full scan modes
- ownership verification and consent flow
- async jobs with status, events, and artifacts
- public limitations before launch

- [English scanner foundation](https://github.com/Gudvin82/seo-geo-ai-roadmap/blob/main/docs/en/public-scanner-v360.md)
- [Russian scanner foundation](https://github.com/Gudvin82/seo-geo-ai-roadmap/blob/main/docs/ru/public-scanner-v360.md)
