# Answer-Ready Patterns

## Core structure

1. First sentence gives the direct answer.
2. Next paragraph explains context or scope.
3. Lists, tables, and short blocks help extraction.
4. Proof, caveats, and next actions keep trust intact.

## Before / after pattern

- Weak: long intro, vague framing, answer hidden below the fold
- Better: direct answer, supporting detail, evidence, clear CTA

## Page types that benefit most

- service pages
- FAQ pages
- comparison pages
- local landing pages
- founder or expert profile pages
