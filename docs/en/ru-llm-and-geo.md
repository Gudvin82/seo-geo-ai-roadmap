# RU LLM and GEO

## Practical scope

For Russian-language GEO/AI work it is useful to account for:

- YandexGPT
- GigaChat
- Yandex Neuro
- broader LLM surfaces with RU browsing or RU content tendencies

## What often matters more in RU

- VK, Dzen, and Telegram as brand-context channels
- local media, directories, maps, and forums
- regional proof and commercial factors
- stronger influence of Yandex ecosystem signals

## What stays universal

- technical SEO remains foundational
- entity and fact consistency still matter
- answer-ready structure still improves extraction
