# Product Surfaces v4.0.0

The repo is now intentionally split into three surfaces:

- `scanner surface`: URL-first intake, gated active scans, and self-serve lead or client entry
- `product/app surface`: dashboards, executive reporting, integrations, graph intelligence, and task export
- `repo/docs/operator surface`: prompts, scripts, contracts, architecture, and release discipline

This split is mirrored in:

- `README` and `README_RU`
- `START_HERE_FOR_AI*`
- `/api/v1/settings/product-modes`
- scanner, app, and graph frontend entrypoints
