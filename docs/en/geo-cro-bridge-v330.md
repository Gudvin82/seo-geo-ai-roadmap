# GEO to CRO Bridge

Discoverability is not the finish line. AI-ready pages still need to convert.

## Patterns that help both extraction and conversion

- short factual summaries above the fold
- expert and proof blocks near claims
- comparison sections
- FAQ blocks with decision-stage questions
- clear CTA placement after trust-building context

## Anti-pattern

Do not flatten the page into a machine-only answer block. If a page becomes easy
to extract but hard for a human to trust, the business outcome degrades.
