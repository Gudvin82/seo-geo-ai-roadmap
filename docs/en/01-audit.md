# Starting Audit

> **Tags:** [U] [AI/GEO]
> **Priority:** High
> **Roadmap phase:** Month 0-1

## Why this matters

Audit work gives the baseline for prioritization. Without it, teams fix random issues instead of the blockers that affect crawlability, rankings, conversions, and AI visibility.

## Step-by-step

1. Review indexing, status codes, canonicals, and XML sitemaps.
2. Check page speed, mobile UX, internal links, and duplicate clusters.
3. Audit money pages for trust, CTA, pricing, and proof.
4. Test if content is readable without JavaScript.
5. Review AI surfaces: brand mentions, citations, hallucinations, and bot access.

## Checklist

- [ ] Technical audit complete
- [ ] Content audit complete
- [ ] Commercial audit complete
- [ ] AI/GEO audit complete
- [ ] Baseline documented

## AI Prompt

```text
Audit this website for technical SEO, content quality, conversion readiness, and AI discoverability. Return issues grouped by severity and expected business impact.
```

## Example

A site may rank poorly not because of content quality, but because service pages are blocked by `noindex`, hidden behind JS rendering, and lack trust proof.
