# 30-Day GEO Launch Playbook

## Goal

Launch a practical GEO/AI baseline without pretending that one month creates a
fully governed system.

## Inputs

- current site and business goals
- priority pages
- access to analytics, crawl data, and one AI visibility workflow
- one operator owner for the cycle

## Weekly plan

1. Week 1: baseline technical SEO, facts, and AI surface checks.
2. Week 2: patch homepage, service pages, FAQ, and structured data.
3. Week 3: publish `llms.txt`, review AI bot access, and improve trust assets.
4. Week 4: recheck AI SoV proxies, compare deltas, and plan next cycle.

## Expected artifacts

- baseline worksheet
- patch backlog
- updated core pages
- first GEO/AI report
- one operator summary with bounded next steps

## What to measure

- technical blockers reduced
- answer-ready coverage on money pages
- AI SoV or citation proxy movement
- factual consistency across homepage, about, and FAQ

## Risks

- trying to optimize every page in the first month
- mixing SEO fixes and GEO experiments without tracking evidence separately
- claiming pipeline impact before enough cycles exist

## Minimum viable path

If the team only has one week of execution capacity:

1. fix technical blockers on the homepage and one money page
2. align brand facts
3. add answer-ready FAQ block
4. publish `llms.txt`
5. document the baseline and recheck later

## What not to promise

- stable AI citations in 30 days
- full causality to pipeline in one cycle
