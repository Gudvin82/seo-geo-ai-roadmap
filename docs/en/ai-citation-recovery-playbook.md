# AI Citation Recovery Playbook

## Goal

Recover when the brand disappears, degrades, or is misquoted in monitored AI
answers.

## Inputs

- before/after answer snapshots
- affected queries
- priority pages and brand facts
- known recent changes to site content, schema, or provider prompts

## Step sequence

1. Confirm whether the drop is real or just prompt or surface volatility.
2. Check fact drift across homepage, about, FAQ, offer, and policy pages.
3. Strengthen direct-answer blocks and source clarity.
4. Recheck entity consistency and structured data.
5. Re-run AI SoV and document confidence level.

## Expected outputs

- evidence set with dated before/after snapshots
- page-level patch list
- note on whether the issue was factual, structural, or volatility-driven
- bounded recheck plan

## What to measure

- restored mentions
- cleaner framing
- better source-page alignment
- fewer factual conflicts in repeated prompts

## Risks

- reacting to one unstable answer as if it were a durable trend
- editing ten pages before confirming the root cause
- confusing brand absence with prompt wording mismatch

## What not to promise

- permanent recovery after one page edit
