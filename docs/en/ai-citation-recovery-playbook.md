# AI Citation Recovery Playbook

## Goal

Recover when the brand disappears, degrades, or is misquoted in monitored AI
answers.

## Inputs

- before/after answer snapshots
- affected queries
- priority pages and brand facts

## Steps

1. Confirm whether the drop is real or just prompt/surface volatility.
2. Check fact drift across homepage, about, FAQ, offer, and policy pages.
3. Strengthen direct-answer blocks and source clarity.
4. Recheck entity consistency and structured data.
5. Re-run AI SoV and document confidence level.

## Measure

- restored mentions
- cleaner framing
- better source-page alignment

## Do not promise

- permanent recovery after one page edit
