# GEO Competitive Gap

Use this framework when comparing your site to direct discoverability
competitors.

## Gap types

- entity gap: competitors are easier to resolve as brands or experts
- citation gap: competitors are cited more often on tracked prompts
- factual clarity gap: competitor pages answer with fewer ambiguities
- answer-surface coverage gap: they appear across more assistant surfaces
- multilingual gap: they maintain consistency better across languages

## Comparison template

| Gap | Your state | Competitor state | Risk | First response |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Entity | fragmented | clear | misattribution | fix about, schema, profiles |
| Citation | low mention rate | cited often | invisible in AI answers | improve answer-ready and trust assets |
| Factual clarity | mixed claims | direct facts | quote drift | normalize facts and proof |
| Surface coverage | one surface | three surfaces | narrow discoverability | prioritize pages cited across tools |

## Output

The goal is not to copy competitors blindly. The goal is to spot where their
discoverability system is easier for search and AI systems to interpret.
