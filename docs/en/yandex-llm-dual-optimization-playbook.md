# Yandex + LLM Dual Optimization Playbook

## Goal

Improve both Yandex discoverability and LLM answer readiness without creating
two conflicting site versions.

## Inputs

- RU commercial pages
- Yandex market assumptions
- current FAQ, trust, and fact layers
- AI visibility snapshots for RU prompts

## Step sequence

1. Fix Yandex basics: region, commercial factors, local trust, technical health.
2. Align RU fact pages, offer wording, and FAQ blocks.
3. Publish answer-ready RU sections and structured data.
4. Track Yandex metrics separately from AI visibility proxies.
5. Keep one source of truth for facts across both layers.

## Expected outputs

- Yandex-ready trust baseline
- RU answer-ready page set
- synced fact layer for search and AI
- split reporting: Yandex signals vs AI visibility proxies

## What to measure

- Yandex traffic and quality proxies
- RU query answer clarity
- fewer contradictions in RU fact surfaces

## Risks

- over-optimizing for Yandex snippets while weakening page clarity
- creating one wording for search and another for AI
- ignoring regional trust factors on RU commercial pages

## What not to promise

- that stronger RU facts automatically create stable LLM citations
