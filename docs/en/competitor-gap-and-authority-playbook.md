# Competitor Gap and Authority Playbook

This playbook exists because SEO maturity requires more than internal hygiene.

## Questions to answer

- which competitors own the intent you want
- where they are easier to understand than you
- where they publish stronger proof
- where they win links, mentions, or citations
- which gaps are semantic, technical, factual, or authority-related

## Compare at four layers

1. Page coverage
   Which page types do they have that you do not?
2. Entity clarity
   Are they easier to resolve as a company, expert, service, or source?
3. Proof strength
   Do they show stronger case studies, reviews, trust pages, benchmarks?
4. Distribution and authority
   Do they have stronger links, mentions, communities, or recurring citations?

## What to extract

- competitor page inventory
- topic and intent gaps
- trust and proof gaps
- authority and citation gaps
- “must-build” page list

## Link and authority reality

This repo is not pretending link building is optional.

If a competitor is winning because:

- more sites cite them
- journalists or communities reference them
- they own comparison or category pages
- they have stronger proof assets

then the answer is not only “tune schema”.

It is often:

- build better proof pages
- earn references and links
- improve comparison and category content
- strengthen entity resolution across platforms

## Outputs

- competitor gap matrix
- authority gap list
- page build list
- PR / mention / citation opportunities
- implementation priority by expected business effect
