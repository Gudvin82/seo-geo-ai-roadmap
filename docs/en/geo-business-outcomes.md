# GEO Business Outcomes

## Where GEO/AI connects to business

GEO/AI work matters only when it supports:

- leads
- pipeline creation
- branded demand
- assisted conversions
- trust during evaluation

## Reporting view

| Layer | Example metrics | Business interpretation |
|---|---|---|
| SEO metrics | indexed pages, CTR, non-brand clicks | reach and qualified discovery |
| GEO/AI metrics | AI SoV, AI Citation Score, answer-surface coverage | directional brand visibility in assistants |
| Business metrics | leads, booked calls, influenced opportunities | commercial value and trust |

## Suggested report blocks

1. What changed in technical/search signals.
2. What changed in AI visibility proxies.
3. What changed in lead quality, branded demand, or assisted conversion paths.
4. What is observed versus inferred versus still experimental.

## Safe claim pattern

Use language like:

- “AI visibility improved alongside stronger branded demand”
- “We observed cleaner answer extraction on priority pages”
- “We do not yet claim causal lift until more cycles are measured”
