# GEO Conversion and CRO

GEO pages should not only be citable. They should also help humans convert.

## What works well

- direct answers near commercial intent
- trust proof immediately after the answer
- short path from explanation to CTA
- pages that are both extractable by models and navigable by humans

## Strong page pattern

1. direct answer
2. explanation and scope
3. proof: case, metric, authority, policy
4. CTA that matches the user intent stage

## CRO questions

- If a user lands here from search, can they understand the offer in 10 seconds?
- If an AI cites this page, does the page still create trust and next-step clarity?
- Are the answer block and the CTA aligned, or are they disconnected?
