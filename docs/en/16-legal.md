# Legal and Policy Pages

> **Tags:** [U]
> **Priority:** Medium
> **Roadmap phase:** Month 0-2

## Why this matters

Policy pages help trust, compliance, and indexable completeness for commercial websites.

## Step-by-step

1. Publish privacy, terms, cookies, and consent policies.
2. Add company details and contact transparency.
3. Align policy language with actual data handling.
4. Link policies from footer and forms.
5. Recheck after major product changes.
