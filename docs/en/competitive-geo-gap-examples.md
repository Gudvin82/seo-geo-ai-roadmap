# Competitive GEO Gap Examples

## Example 1: Entity gap

- observed: competitor pages have explicit expert pages, structured service pages, and cited case studies
- inferred: the competitor is easier for LLMs to summarize as a category authority
- hypothesis: your brand will underperform in answer-surface mentions until trust anchors improve

## Example 2: AI citation gap

- observed: competitor appears in AI responses with brand mention plus source URL; your brand does not
- inferred: competitor has stronger answer-ready and trust-ready surfaces
- hypothesis: improvements to `llms.txt`, expert pages, and FAQ structure should raise mention probability
