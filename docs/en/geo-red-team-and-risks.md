# GEO Red-Team and Risks

This document exists to keep GEO work honest.

## Common failure modes

- AI cites the wrong page even when your facts are correct
- a model invents a claim or number that sounds plausible
- teams over-optimize extraction and damage human UX
- visibility grows on weak pages that do not build trust

## What not to do

- do not spam `llms.txt` with hundreds of low-value URLs
- do not create fake “AI pages” with no user need
- do not remove context just to chase snippet extraction
- do not promise controllable citations on volatile answer surfaces

## Red-team checklist

- Can another model misread the same page?
- Do numbers stay consistent across homepage, about, schema, and FAQ?
- If the answer is extracted without context, is it still safe and accurate?
- Is visibility growing on the pages that actually support revenue and trust?

## Visibility without trust

One of the worst patterns is being mentioned often but described badly. Treat
that as a brand-risk issue, not a win.
