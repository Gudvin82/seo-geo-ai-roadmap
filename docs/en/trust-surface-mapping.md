# Trust Surface Mapping

Inventory the surfaces that influence whether search engines, users, and LLMs
see the brand as credible.

## Core surfaces

- reviews
- authorship and expert pages
- case studies
- legal and policy pages
- external profiles
- about and proof pages

## Why it matters

- GEO/AI systems need citation-worthy trust anchors
- humans need proof before conversion
- missing trust surfaces often create both ranking and conversion drag

## Audit template

For each surface, mark:

- present or missing
- current owner
- freshness
- factual consistency with the truth center
- expected effect on AI citation and conversion trust
