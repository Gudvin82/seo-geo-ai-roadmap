# Content Operations

> **Tags:** [U]
> **Priority:** Medium
> **Roadmap phase:** Month 1-12

## Why this matters

Execution fails when teams publish inconsistently or without a shared brief system.

## Step-by-step

1. Standardize briefs and review steps.
2. Separate research, draft, SME review, and publication.
3. Maintain a refresh backlog.
4. Tag pages by funnel stage and intent.
5. Track publish-to-impact time.
