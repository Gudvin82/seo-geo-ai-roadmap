# SERM and Reputation

> **Tags:** [U] [RU]
> **Priority:** Medium
> **Roadmap phase:** Month 2-6

## Why this matters

Search and AI systems mirror the public reputation footprint of a brand.

## Step-by-step

1. Audit branded SERPs and review platforms.
2. Build positive proof assets.
3. Respond to reviews consistently.
4. Fix recurring complaint themes in product or onboarding.
5. Feed high-trust reputation pages into branded SEO.
