# Competitor Analysis

> **Tags:** [U] [AI/GEO]
> **Priority:** High
> **Roadmap phase:** Month 0-1

## Why this matters

Ranking is relative. You win when your site, offer, proof, and machine-readable structure outperform what already gets cited and recommended.

## Step-by-step

1. Collect SERP competitors by cluster.
2. Compare offers, trust elements, and page formats.
3. Track who AI tools cite for target prompts.
4. Note content gaps, proof gaps, and entity gaps.
5. Turn insights into page and asset backlog.
