# Business and Product Strategy

> **Tags:** [U]
> **Priority:** High
> **Roadmap phase:** Month 0-1

## Why this matters

SEO and GEO do not work well when the offer, segment, and conversion goal are vague. Strategy defines what demand to capture and what outcome the channel must create.

## When to apply

Use for any new website, relaunch, market expansion, or SEO recovery project.

## Step-by-step

1. Define audience segments by pain point, budget, region, and maturity.
2. Write JTBD, objections, and value propositions for each segment.
3. Prioritize offers by margin, sales cycle, and search demand.
4. Map offer groups to TOFU, MOFU, and BOFU page types.
5. Set forecast assumptions for traffic, leads, sales, and revenue.

## Checklist

- [ ] ICP and segments defined
- [ ] JTBD and objections documented
- [ ] Offer stack prioritized
- [ ] Funnel page map approved
- [ ] Revenue model aligned with SEO goals

## AI Prompt

```text
Act as a senior SEO strategist. Based on my business description, create audience segments, JTBD, objections, offer hierarchy, and a search-led funnel structure.
```

## Example

For a B2B AI agency: separate founders, operations directors, and marketing leads, then create service pages for implementation, audits, and support retainers.

## Common mistakes

- Starting keyword work before offer strategy
- Mixing all segments into one generic page
