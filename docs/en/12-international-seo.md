# International SEO

> **Tags:** [INT]
> **Priority:** Medium
> **Roadmap phase:** Month 2-6

## Why this matters

Localization is more than translation. Search intent, entities, and SERP expectations differ by market.

## Step-by-step

1. Choose a URL structure for languages and regions.
2. Implement `hreflang` correctly.
3. Localize offers, proof, and examples.
4. Separate market-specific keyword clusters.
5. Track indexation and ranking per locale.
