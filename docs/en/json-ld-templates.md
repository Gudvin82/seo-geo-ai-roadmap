# JSON-LD Templates for GEO and AI

Use the templates in `templates/schema/` as copy-paste starters.

## Included templates

- `organization-schema.json`
- `faq-schema.json`
- `howto-schema.json`
- `product-schema.json`
- `service-schema.json`
- `local-business-schema.json`

## How to adapt them

- replace placeholder names, URLs, identifiers, and prices
- keep claims aligned with visible page content
- connect organization, service, product, and local pages back to the same entity
- do not add schema for content that the page itself does not support

## GEO/AI benefit

These templates reduce ambiguity. They do not guarantee citations, but they
make it easier for search systems and models to map the right entity and page.
