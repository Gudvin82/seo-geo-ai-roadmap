# Entity SEO and Knowledge Graph

## Objective

Help search systems and LLMs map one brand, one person, or one product to one
coherent entity.

## Core building blocks

- canonical fact pages
- consistent name, URL, and profile references
- structured data
- internal hubs that connect offers, experts, company facts, and trust assets
- off-site references that reinforce the same identity

## Practical sources

- Wikidata or public entity references where relevant
- NAP consistency for local businesses
- official profiles, media mentions, and author pages
- `llms.txt` and `ai.txt` as route maps, not as replacements for facts
