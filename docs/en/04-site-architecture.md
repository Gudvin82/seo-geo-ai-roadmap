# Site Architecture

> **Tags:** [U] [INT]
> **Priority:** High
> **Roadmap phase:** Month 0-1

## Why this matters

Architecture decides crawl depth, topical clarity, and how well pages map to search intent.

## Step-by-step

1. Define required page types.
2. Group pages by topic, geography, industry, and funnel stage.
3. Keep URL logic predictable and scalable.
4. Build internal linking around parent-child relationships.
5. Reserve space for FAQs, comparison pages, and glossary assets.
