# v4.6.0 Release Summary

`v4.6.0` turns the integration layer into a fuller executive stack.

## New first-class integrations

- Google Ads
- IndexNow
- Google Business Profile
- Yandex Business
- Merchant Center
- Meta Ads
- VK Ads
- Telegram ads or channel analytics
- YouTube Analytics
- LinkedIn Ads
- Instagram or Facebook organic analytics

## What this changes

- Google stack can now be interpreted as one executive layer:
  GSC + GA4 + Google Ads + CrUX
- RU stack can now be interpreted as one executive layer:
  Yandex Webmaster + Yandex Metrica + Yandex Direct + Yandex Business
- Local SEO and commerce now have a clearer starter path through
  Google Business Profile, Yandex Business, and Merchant Center
- Distribution data is now explicitly separated from core search truth through
  Meta, VK, Telegram, YouTube, LinkedIn, and Instagram or Facebook organic

## Executive metrics now highlighted

- organic demand
- paid demand
- AI visibility
- landing-page conversion
- CTR
- CPA
- CPL
- local trust and review signals

## Product interpretation

This release makes the repo stronger as a self-hosted operating system and
service foundation. It still does not claim that maintainers run a hosted SaaS
for you.
