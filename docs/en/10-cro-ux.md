# CRO and UX

> **Tags:** [U]
> **Priority:** High
> **Roadmap phase:** Month 1-6

## Why this matters

Traffic without conversion is wasted budget. UX decides whether SEO demand becomes pipeline.

## Step-by-step

1. Clarify one main CTA per page.
2. Add trust blocks, cases, pricing hints, and objections.
3. Improve mobile forms and interaction speed.
4. Test CTA copy and lead magnet offers.
5. Review call recordings or form-drop behavior.
