# Product Modes v3.8.0

To avoid looking like "everything at once", `v3.8.0` separates three first-class
surfaces:

- Repo methodology mode
- App control panel mode
- Scanner intake mode

Machine-readable source:

- `GET /api/v1/settings/product-modes`

The goal is clarity:

- the repo teaches and routes
- the app operates
- the scanner intakes and exports
