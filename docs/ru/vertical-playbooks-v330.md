# Vertical Playbooks

## Legal

- priority signals: expertise, authorship, jurisdiction, policy clarity
- common mistakes: thin service pages, vague claims, weak proof
- AI risk: hallucinated guarantees or legal advice inflation

## Medical

- priority signals: medical review, citations, safety disclaimers
- common mistakes: promotional overclaiming
- AI risk: unsafe summarization or omitted caveats

## B2B SaaS

- priority signals: category clarity, comparisons, integrations, proof blocks
- common mistakes: feature-first pages with weak business framing
- AI risk: model confusion about ICP, use case или pricing model

## Local Services

- priority signals: region proof, contact consistency, reviews, entity clarity
- common mistakes: weak service-area signals
- AI risk: wrong city, availability или operating-hours extraction

## E-commerce

- priority signals: availability, policy pages, shipping, returns, reviews
- common mistakes: weak structured data and trust surfaces
- AI risk: stale product facts или missing policy context
