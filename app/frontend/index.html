    Operator overview

    First result in 15 minutes

    1. Sign in and create one workspace.
    2. Create one project and fill brand facts.
    3. Connect one provider or stay in transparent starter mode.
    4. Run one audit and one AI SoV check.
    5. Open reports, artifacts, and export package.

    What changes in 30 days

    • You have a repeatable audit rhythm, not one-off checklists.
    • Facts, prompts, and reports stay in one operator system.
    • You can show deltas in score, evidence, and AI visibility.

    What changes in 90 days

    • Agency, in-house, and founder workflows become reusable.
    • You have a bilingual evidence base for fixes and client delivery.
    • AI SoV, audits, and truth-center governance start compounding.

    Who this is for / not for

    For
    • Agencies running recurring audits and client reports.
    • In-house teams that need one SEO + GEO + AI operating layer.
    • Founders and expert operators shipping their own sites.
    Not for
    • Teams expecting a black-box crawler with no review.
    • Users who want a mandatory hosted SaaS.
    • Anyone looking for GEO snake-oil without technical SEO discipline.
