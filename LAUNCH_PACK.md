# Launch Pack

This file is the shortest safe launch narrative for the repository.

## Safe public positioning

- free self-hosted SEO, GEO, and AI discoverability platform
- AI-agent-ready repo with app layer, scripts, contracts, and proofs
- foundation for your own audit or scanner service

## Avoid

- hosted SaaS operated by the maintainer
- guaranteed rankings or guaranteed AI citations
- fully autonomous site changes with no human review
