# Commercial Roadmap

## What stays free and open-source

- repository docs, prompts, scripts, templates, and checklists
- self-hosted app foundation
- local deployment model
- transparent scoring and operator logic

## What may become commercial later

- hosted convenience layer
- support, SLA, onboarding, and migration services
- managed integrations and enterprise operations support

## What is not promised today

- always-on hosted SaaS
- enterprise SSO/SCIM
- managed infra or support contract by default

The commercial path must not break the self-hosted-first position.
