# Self-Hosted Use Cases

This platform is free, transparent, and self-hosted first.

## For yourself

- run discoverability audits on your own sites
- keep all data in your own infrastructure
- adapt prompts, checklists, reports, and local or cloud AI providers

## For your team

- standardize SEO, GEO, and AI-audit workflows
- keep artifacts exportable
- avoid mandatory vendor lock-in
- invite editors, admins, and viewers into shared workspaces

## For your agency

- deploy internally for operators
- use white-label workspace titles and report framing
- adapt the app for client-facing internal workflows
- connect client-specific providers or local LLM environments

## For your own product or service

- fork it
- extend it
- build a free or commercial service on top
- keep ownership of deployment and data
