# Differentiators

## Core differentiators

1. Bilingual by design
English and Russian are both first-class operating layers with shared structure and different market priorities.

2. Google + Yandex + LLM in one framework
The framework connects traditional search, Russian-speaking market specifics, and AI answer surfaces.

3. Execution-first structure
Every major topic is backed by SOPs, checklists, prompts, templates, scripts, and examples.

4. Actionable operating system, not a reading list
The repository is meant to help teams make decisions, assign owners, validate changes, and report outcomes.

5. AI-native layer
`llms.txt`, AI bot access, answer-ready content, hallucination fixes, and AI visibility monitoring are included from the start.

6. Governance-ready delivery
RACI, Definition of Done, implementation roadmap, release discipline, and reporting templates support real team execution.

7. Discoverability framing wider than SEO
The system covers search, local platforms, AI surfaces, reputation, and multilingual presence.

8. Practical value for agencies and in-house teams
The repository is structured so the same playbooks can support audits, retainers, launches, and internal operating routines.
