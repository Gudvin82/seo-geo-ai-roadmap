# Glossary

## SEO

Search Engine Optimization: improving technical, structural, and content signals
so pages earn relevant search visibility.

See also: [docs/en/05-technical-seo.md](./docs/en/05-technical-seo.md)

## GEO

Generative Engine Optimization: shaping content and signals so AI assistants and
answer engines can discover, extract, and recommend a brand correctly.

See also: [docs/en/08-geo-ai-search.md](./docs/en/08-geo-ai-search.md)

## AI discoverability

The broader ability of a brand or page to be found, cited, or recommended
inside AI systems and AI-influenced search flows.

## AI Share of Voice

A tracking lens for how often and how well a brand appears across AI platforms
for a defined prompt set.

See also: [examples/ai-share-of-voice-weekly-report.md](./examples/ai-share-of-voice-weekly-report.md)

## llms.txt

A lightweight text file that helps AI systems understand important public pages
and the structure of a site.

See also: [templates/llms.txt.example](./templates/llms.txt.example)

## llms-full.txt

An expanded variant of `llms.txt` that can include more context, coverage, and
site structure details.

See also: [templates/llms-full.txt.example](./templates/llms-full.txt.example)

## AI bots

Automated agents such as GPTBot, ChatGPT-User, PerplexityBot, or Bingbot that
fetch or interpret web content.

See also: [scripts/check-robots-ai-bots.py](./scripts/check-robots-ai-bots.py)

## answer-ready content

Content formatted so a human or LLM can extract the core answer quickly without
guesswork.

See also: [prompts/en/answer-ready-page-prompt.md](./prompts/en/answer-ready-page-prompt.md)

## E-E-A-T

Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, and Trustworthiness: a quality lens
for content and entities.

See also: [docs/en/07-content-eeat.md](./docs/en/07-content-eeat.md)

## entity authority

The strength and consistency of a brand, person, or organization as a known
entity across site content and external citations.

See also: [templates/entity-authority-profile-template.md](./templates/entity-authority-profile-template.md)

## Yandex commercial factors

Trust, transaction, regional, and business signals that influence commercial
performance in Yandex.

See also: [checklists/en/yandex-commercial-factors-checklist.md](./checklists/en/yandex-commercial-factors-checklist.md)

## local SEO

Optimization focused on geography, maps, NAP consistency, local proof, and
regional demand capture.

See also: [docs/en/11-local-seo.md](./docs/en/11-local-seo.md)

## programmatic SEO

Scalable page creation based on structured data, templates, and repeatable
query patterns.

## CRO

Conversion Rate Optimization: improving how traffic becomes leads, revenue, or
pipeline.

See also: [docs/en/10-cro-ux.md](./docs/en/10-cro-ux.md)

## DoD

Definition of Done: the minimum completion criteria for a task, page, or
release before it can be considered finished.

See also: [docs/en/21-definition-of-done.md](./docs/en/21-definition-of-done.md)

## RACI

Responsible, Accountable, Consulted, Informed: a governance framework for clear
ownership and approvals.

See also: [docs/en/20-raci.md](./docs/en/20-raci.md)
