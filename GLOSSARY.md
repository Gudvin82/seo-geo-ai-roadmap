# Glossary

## SEO

Technical, structural, and content work that improves search visibility.

## GEO

Generative Engine Optimization: the work of making a site easier for AI systems
to find, extract, and describe correctly.

## AI discoverability

The broader layer above SEO where a brand can be found, cited, summarized, or
recommended in AI-assisted answer flows.

## AI Share of Voice

A repeatable monitoring view of how often and how well a brand appears across
AI providers for a fixed prompt set.

## AI Citation Score

A transparent `0-100` score derived from structured AI SoV results. It is a
trend signal, not a hidden black-box metric.

## Brand facts

The canonical truth layer for brand, legal, numeric, market, and language
claims.

## Factual consistency

The practice of keeping public copy, metadata, schema, reports, and AI-facing
files aligned around the same verifiable truth.

## Truth center

The operational home for canonical brand facts and allowed claims.

## llms.txt

A public text file that helps AI systems discover important pages and site
structure.

## Entity clarity

How clearly a site separates its founder, brand, products, services, and sibling
entities.
